About Our Department

Established in 2020 • Excellence for 5 Years

Department Overview

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering (AI & ML) at Pimpri Chinchwad University stands as a beacon of technological innovation and academic excellence. Established with a vision to nurture the next generation of tech leaders, our department combines cutting-edge research with industry-aligned curriculum to create globally competent engineers.
We are committed to fostering an environment where students can explore, innovate, and excel in the rapidly evolving fields of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Computer Science. Our state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and industry partnerships provide students with unparalleled opportunities for growth and development.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized center of excellence in Computer Science & Engineering, specializing in AI & ML, that produces innovative leaders and researchers who contribute to technological advancement and societal progress.

Our Mission

To provide world-class education, foster research and innovation, and develop ethical, socially responsible engineers equipped with cutting-edge knowledge in AI, ML, and emerging technologies to meet global industry demands.
