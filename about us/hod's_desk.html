HoD's Desk

Dr. Swati Shirke-Deshmukh

Head, Department of Computer Science & Engineering (AI & ML),
Pimpri Chinchwad University

I am thrilled to welcome you to the Department of Computer Science & Engineering (AI & ML) at Pimpri Chinchwad University. Our department is dedicated to fostering the growth of young individuals by equipping them with the necessary knowledge, skills, and values to thrive in the fast-paced technological landscape of today.

We emphasize not only a solid foundation in computer science but also the development of expertise in innovative fields such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Blockchain, Cybersecurity, Data Science, and Cloud Computing. With a passionate team of faculty, modern infrastructure, and partnerships with industry, our department works to cultivate an engaging educational environment that combines academic rigor with practical experience.

At PCU, we prioritize hands-on learning, research-based teaching, and interdisciplinary projects that enable students to innovate and address real-world issues. We encourage our students to take part in hackathons, research endeavors, technical symposiums, and professional organizations such as IEEE and ACM to extend their learning beyond the classroom.

As the Head of the Department, my goal is to cultivate globally competent engineers who excel in technology while also embodying ethical principles, social responsibility, and leadership skills. We strive to prepare our graduates to be industry-ready while promoting an entrepreneurial spirit and a commitment to lifelong learning.

I encourage you to discover the opportunities our department has to offer and join us in our mission to build a future driven by innovation, sustainability, and human values.

Together, let us shape minds, transform lives, and create impactful leaders of tomorrow.

Best Regards,
Dr. Swati Shirke-Deshmukh
