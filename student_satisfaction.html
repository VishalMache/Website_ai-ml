Additional Information

The AI/ML department not only focuses on academic excellence but also ensures holistic student development. Students are provided with state-of-the-art computer labs, access to premium software tools, and opportunities to work on real-world projects that bridge the gap between theory and practice. Regular workshops, coding competitions, and hackathons are organized to encourage innovation and problem-solving skills.

The department actively supports students in publishing research papers, presenting at conferences, and participating in national and international technical events. Dedicated placement training sessions, mock interviews, and resume-building workshops further prepare students for career opportunities in top companies. We encourage students to stay connected with us through our official website, social media channels, and alumni groups, so that they remain part of our ever-growing community of innovators and professionals in the AI/ML domain.
